Kasturi Methi

Kasturi Methi

Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves) adds a rich aroma and slight bitterness to Indian dishes like curries and breads. It's packed with fiber, iron, and antioxidants.

Garam Masala

Garam Masala

Garam masala is a fragrant spice blend used in Indian cuisine, made from cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, cumin, coriander, and more. It adds warmth and depth to dishes.

Coriander Powder

Coriander Powder

Coriander powder is made by grinding dried coriander seeds. It is an essential spice in many cuisines, especially Indian, Middle Eastern, and Latin American cooking.

Turmeric Powder

Turmeric Powder

Turmeric powder is a bright yellow spice commonly used in Indian cooking, especially in dishes like curries, rice, and soups. It has a warm, earthy flavor with slight bitterness and is known for its health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

Red Chilli Powder

Red Chilli Powder

Red chili powder is a spice made by grinding dried red chili peppers, often used to add heat and color to various dishes, and can be a pure chili powder or a blend with other spices.

Cardamom Powder

Cardamom Powder

Cardamom, also known as "elaichi" in Hindi, is a spice made from the seeds of various plants in the ginger family (Zingiberaceae), known for its unique, warm, and aromatic flavor. It is commonly used in both sweet and savory dishes and has medicinal properties.

Black Loose Leaf

Black Loose Leaf

"Black loose leaf tea" refers to tea made from the Camellia sinensis plant, where the leaves are fully oxidized and sold in their whole or partially broken leaf form, rather than being pre-packaged in tea bags.
