Meet the Chef

Sanju Khatri

Sanju (Dinesh) Khatri is a talented Indian chef trained in traditional Indian cuisine. Sanju has many years of experience in the kitchen and amazing attention to detail. Even over a screen, Sanju is able to accurately assist in creating a delicious, authentic dish. Through clear instruction and an impressive eye for color and texture, his advice is a priceless tool to help you achieve authentic Indian cuisine in your own home. Sanju was born and raised in Uttarakhand, a beautiful northern state of India that borders Nepal. He currently works as the chef at Namastay Himalayas Homestay in the foothills of the Himalayas. Sanju speaks four languages, and lucky for us, English is one of them.
